## ğŸ†˜ Common Questions

### Q: Can I delete an order?
**A:** You can cancel orders, but not delete them (for audit trail). Change status to "Cancelled".

### Q: What happens when I create an order?
**A:** The system automatically:
- Reduces product stock
- Creates an invoice
- Updates customer stats
- Adds to recent activity

### Q: How do I know when to reorder products?
**A:** Check the Dashboard for low stock alerts. Products with less than 10 units show as "Low Stock".

### Q: Can multiple people use the system?
**A:** Yes! Each person gets their own login with appropriate permissions based on their role.

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes! All passwords are encrypted, and the system uses JWT tokens for secure authentication.

### Q: Can I export data?
**A:** Yes! Most modules have export options for reports and data backup.
